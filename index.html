About the Scholar

As a distinguished student pursuing an Integrated Master of Technology at the esteemed Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology, I dedicate myself to the noble pursuit of merging artificial intelligence with the foundational principles of system-level development.

My scholarly endeavors encompass the intricate realm of operating systems development, the art of cybersecurity, and the eloquent language of Python programming. Through meticulous study and practical application, I have crafted projects that span from bespoke operating systems to sophisticated voice-driven assistants.

I am an ardent contributor to the venerable tradition of open-source Linux and embedded systems projects, with particular research interests in the ancient craft of reverse engineering and the fortification of kernel security. My aspiration is to contribute meaningfully to impactful software development initiatives and the ever-evolving landscape of emerging technologies.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have the distinguished honor of serving as Managing Director of Sheen Shawls, our family's cherished enterprise specializing in the preservation and creation of antique Pashmina shawls. Through this role, I endeavor to maintain the legacy of authentic, handcrafted Pashmina artistry that has been passed down through generations.
